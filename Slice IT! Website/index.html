What is Slice IT!
A game that is associated with shapes and colors. There are five levels in total.

The player reads a Boolean statement at the top of the screen and needs to slice the shape/color that makes the statement true.

If player slices a shape/color that makes the statement false then the level restarts.

Player also needs to watch out for bombs or the game will restart if accidentally sliced.
To illustrate STEM concepts

To represent programming fundamentals

Teach logical expressions

Bring interest in Information Technology!
